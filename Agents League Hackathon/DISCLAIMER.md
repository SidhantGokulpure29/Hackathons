# Disclaimer

CrisisBrief is a hackathon prototype. It is not a replacement for professional incident command, legal review, security review, or executive judgment.

The seeded incident evidence is synthetic. Generated briefs should be reviewed by qualified humans before operational use.

When Azure AI Foundry credentials are configured, uploaded evidence may be sent to the configured Azure endpoint for analysis. Users are responsible for choosing appropriate data, access controls, and retention settings.
