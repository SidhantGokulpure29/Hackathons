# Incident Communication Policy

SEV-1 incidents require an internal incident commander, engineering lead, support lead, and executive stakeholder.

For customer-impacting checkout or payment incidents, the public status page must be updated within 20 minutes of SEV-1 declaration.

Status page updates must repeat every 30 minutes until the incident is mitigated.

Customer communications must distinguish confirmed facts from hypotheses and must not blame a vendor or internal team before root cause is verified.

Post-incident review must include timeline, customer impact, contributing factors, corrective actions, and evidence links.
