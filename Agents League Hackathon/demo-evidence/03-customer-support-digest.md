# Customer Support Digest

Between 09:13 UTC and 10:35 UTC, support received 184 customer complaints about failed checkout and duplicate payment uncertainty.

Enterprise merchant Northstar Retail reported that customers could not complete orders in East US after entering card details.

Merchant Velora Market reported failed checkout sessions in West Europe and requested a written incident summary for their operations team.

Support tagged the issue as payment outage, checkout failure, and revenue-impacting because customers abandoned carts after repeated authorization timeouts.

No evidence in the support digest confirms exact revenue loss.
